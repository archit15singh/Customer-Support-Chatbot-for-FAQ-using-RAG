Recognize the signs Judging behavior based on online posts alone is challenging, but there are potential warning signs or indicators that someone may be thinking about self-harm or suicide.Below are questions that you may ask yourself to help assess if an individual may be experiencing suicidal thoughts: Does this person frequently post content about depression or feelings of hopelessness? Is this person posting comments about death or feelings that death is the only option? Are they posting comments about having attempted suicide in the past? Are they describing or posting photos of self-harm or identifying themselves as suicidal? Has their mood and the content of their posts changed recently?
