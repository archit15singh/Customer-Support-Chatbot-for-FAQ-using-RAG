How to add your location to a Post Skip to main content Help Center How to add your location to a Post Note: Turning on this feature allows X to show the location you are posting from as part of your post.You can choose to add a location to your posts using X for Android, X for iOS, X.com or other mobile applications.For example: To give additional location context to your post, you can add a general location label such as âSoMa, San Francisco." In select locations, on X for iOS and X for Android, you may also label your post with the name of a specific business, landmark, or other point of interest.These locations are provided by Foursquare and Yelp.If you are using X for Android or X for iOS, the post may also include your precise location (i.e., the GPS coordinates from which you posted), which can be found through the X API, in addition to the location label you select.See below for more information.Posting with your location To add a location to your Posts: Step 1 Once precise location is enabled on your device, compose a Post as you normally would.If you haven't previously attached a location to your Posts, you may see a prompt asking if youâd like to enable precise location.Step 2 Tap the location icon in the Post compose box to open a list of places that you can choose.Step 3 Select the location you want to add to your Post. Step 4 If youâre attaching a photo to your Post that was taken on the in-app camera and tap the location icon, your precise location (latitude and longitude) will be associated with the Post and findable via API. Step 5 For earlier versions of X for iOS, your Post will always contain both your selected location name and the precise location of your device (which can be found via API) when you Post. Step 6 The next time you Post using the X app on the same device, your general location will automatically be displayed with your Post, using your current location.For X for iOS versions before 6.26, your precise location will automatically be attached to the Post (and findable through API) along with the general location. To stop including your location in your Posts: Step 1 Tap the Post compose icon.Step 2 Tap the location icon to open the dropdown list of locations.Step 3 Your current location will be highlighted.Tap X in the top left to remove your location information from the Post.Your location information will not appear in future Posts until you later choose to add location to a Post.
