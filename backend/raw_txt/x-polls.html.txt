You can vote in a poll one time.The current total vote count and the amount of time remaining in the poll are displayed under the poll choices. To view final results: A X Poll ends between 5 minutes and 7 days after it has been posted, depending on the duration set by the person who posted it.The winning choice is shown in bold.If you vote in a poll, you may receive a push notification alerting you to the final results.
