No, Bookmarks are private and are only viewable to you within your X account. Are Bookmarks the same as Lists? No, Lists are comprised of posts from other accounts by group, topic, or interest, and are discoverable.Learn more about how to use Lists. Can I use Bookmarks in Spaces?
