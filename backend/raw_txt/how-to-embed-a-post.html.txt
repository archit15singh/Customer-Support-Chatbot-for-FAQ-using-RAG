How to embed a post on your website or blog Skip to main content Help Center How to embed a post on your website or blog How to embed a Post on your website or blog Click the icon located within the post.From the menu, select Embed post.This will open publish.x.com where you can customize the look of the embedded post by clicking set customization options.If the post is a reply to another post, you can check Hide Conversation to hide the original post.Once you like the look of the embedded post, copy the code provided by clicking the Copy Code button.Paste the code into your blog or website.Note: Embedding posts is only available via X.com. Examples of embedded Posts Single Post: Reply including original Post: Post including media: Frequently asked questions Can I embed a Post that contains a photo, video, or other media? Yes! An embedded Post can display photos and videos uploaded to X as well as content described by a link.What if the Post I want to embed is part of a conversation (a reply)? When the embedded Post is a reply, the parent Post will be displayed above the reply.A permalink will be available on both Posts to the reply, and a link to the replierâs profile will be available on the reply.Check the Hide Conversation checkbox when setting customization options on publish.x.com to hide the parent Post.Can I embed a Post from someone who chooses to protect their Posts? Embed code is not available for Posts from people who choose to protect their Posts. What if someone deletes a Post, changes their Posts from public to protected, or the account becomes suspended after the Post has been embedded on another website? Text content of the Post will still be visible, but media will not be loaded by X's JavaScript. Visit our developer site for more information about embedded Posts.
