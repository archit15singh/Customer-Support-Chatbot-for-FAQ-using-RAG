How to use X via SMS or text | X Help Skip to main content Help Center How to use your SMS-created account on web When you sign up for X from your phone, we collect all of your updates on the web.Ready to activate your account on X.com? All you have to do is add an email address and a password to complete your profile and log in. Go to https://X.com/login.Click the link that says Activate your account , next to Already using X via text message? Enter your phone number when prompted.X will send a verification code to your phone.When you get that text message, enter the code.You'll then be asked to create an account by entering your email address , a password , and a name for your account.Your username will already be entered, since you created this when you signed up via SMS.Click Create my account , and you're all set!
