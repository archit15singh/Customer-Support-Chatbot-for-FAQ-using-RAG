The post activity dashboard is a tool you can use to learn more about your posts and how they resonate with your audience. Use the activity dashboard to: See how people engage with your posts in real time.Compare your post activity and followers, and see how they trend over time.Click on any post to get a detailed view of the number of Reposts, replies, likes, follows, or clicks it receives.Get detailed insights into who your audience is, especially those who engage with your posts.Download your post metrics. To get started accessing your post activity, log in to analytics.twitter.com with your X username and password. How to access the dashboard From the X for iOS app: Make sure you have installed the latest version of X for iPhone or iPad. Tap the analytics icon visible in your posts.
