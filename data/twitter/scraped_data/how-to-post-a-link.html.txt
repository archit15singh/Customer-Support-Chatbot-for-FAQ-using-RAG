How to post links in a Post                                All links (URLs)
posted in posts are shortened using our  t.co service.When viewing the post on
x.com the original URL (or shortened version of the original URL) will be
displayed.Below are instructions on how to post a link in a post on the web and
on your mobile device.How to post a link in a Post
Step 1                Open your preferred mobile app, or begin a new text
message.Step 2                Type or paste the URL into your Post.   Links will
be adjusted with X's link shortener wherever you post them.Some clients will
adjust your character count as you compose a Post; some won't.Step 3
Post.Step 1                Open your preferred mobile app, or begin a new text
message.Step 2                Type or paste the URL into your Post.   Links will
be adjusted with X's link shortener wherever you post them.Some clients will
adjust your character count as you compose a Post; some won't.Step 3
Post.Step 1                Type or paste the URL into the Post box on  
x.com.Step 2                A URL of any length will be altered to 23
characters,  even if the link itself is less than 23 characters long.Your
character count will reflect this.Step 3                Click the Post button to
post your  Post  and link.     Note:  You cannot opt out of our t.co link
shortening service.Why?   Learn more here.URL safety tips         Some URLs can
be harmful.Please be careful when clicking links, especially ones that were
shortened using an external link shortening service.URLs converted by X's link
service are checked against a list of potentially dangerous sites, and when
there's a match, users will be warned before they continue.Always  check to see
what website you're on before giving out your password or downloading any
software.Our  Safe posting help page  has more information on keeping your
account secure.Share this article                                    Post
