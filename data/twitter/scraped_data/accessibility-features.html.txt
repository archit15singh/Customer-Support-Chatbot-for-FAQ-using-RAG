Accessibility features of X                                In 2020, X created a
team dedicated to making the X app accessible to people with disabilities.While
the work to increase accessibility continues, below is a list of the
accessibility features of X.This list is updated quarterly and includes features
that could be considered usability best practices.What we've done

descriptions.Write in sentence case (instead of all caps or all lowercase).Write
