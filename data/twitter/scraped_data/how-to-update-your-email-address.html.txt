How to update your email address                                Having an up-to-
date email address attached to your account is a great step toward improved
account security.Note:   Each time the email address associated with your X
account is updated, we will send an email notification to the previously-used
email address alerting you of this change.For more information on these types of
alerts, read  about account security.  In addition, we will continue to store
your previously-used email addresses, and will use this information for safety
and security purposes.You can access a complete history of the email addresses
associated with your account by downloading your data through  Your X
Data.Update your email address                                       Step 1
Tap the   navigation menu  icon, then tap  Settings and privacy.Step 2
Tap  Account.Step 3                Tap  Email.Step 4                Insert your
email address and tap  Done.     Note : An email address can only be associated
with one X account at a time.Step 1                In the top menu, you will

  Email.Step 5                Type your email address into the  Email  field.
