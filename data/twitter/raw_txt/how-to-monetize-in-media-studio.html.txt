Media Studio Monetization                                X offers two video
monetization programs,  Amplify Pre-roll , and  Amplify Sponsorships.Amplify
Pre-roll         The Amplify Pre-roll program is an opt-in advertising program
to serve pre-roll against your premium video content shared on X.The program was
created to make it easy for publishers to monetize video content on X, and also
turnkey for advertisers to align with brand-safe content.Those partners that are
accepted into the program and develop a sponsorship package will have access to
monetization functionality in Media Studio.Find more information about this
program  here.     Amplify Sponsorships         The Amplify Sponsorships program
allows you to align your premium video content via a one-to-one sponsorship with
an advertiser (or many).Sponsorships are the best way to drive meaningful reach
and revenue for your brand and deliver premium alignment for advertisers who
want to reach your audience.Those partners that are accepted into the program
and develop a sponsorship package will have additional monetization
functionality in Media Studio.Find more information about this program
here.Monetizing with Amplify Pre-roll How-to FAQs   Monetizing with Amplify
Sponsorships How-to FAQs                       Monetizing with Amplify Pre-roll
    To learn more about the Amplify Pre-roll program, reach out to your X
account manager.How to monetize videos                    Click on a video
within your Media Studio library.Click on the  Settings  tab.Click the  Monetize
this video  button.Select a relevant content category in the  How would you
describe your video?  section.If you do not want certain categories of
advertising to appear in front of your video, you can select them in the
Exclude tags  section.Avoid selecting too many tags; this can restrict the
advertising that could be matched to your video.If you do not want certain
advertisers associated with your video, you can manually enter these usernames
in the  Exclude advertiser @handles  section.How to set default monetization
settings                    Click on the   Monetization   button within the top
navigation bar.Click on   Settings   within the dropdown that appears.Enable the
  Monetize all new videos with Amplify Pre-roll   if you would like all newly-
uploaded videos monetized automatically.Select a relevant content category in
the   Tag your content   section.If you do not want certain categories of
advertising to appear in front of your video, you can select them in the  
Exclude advertiser categories   section.Avoid selecting too many tags; this can
restrict the advertising that could be matched to your video.If you do not want
certain advertisers associated with your video, you can manually enter these
usernames in the   Exclude advertiser @handles  section.Click   Save.All new
media set to monetize will have these associated settings.How to view estimated
earnings per video                    Click on a video within your Media Studio
library.Click the   More analytics   button.Select a date range using the filter
at the top right (which defaults to a 7 day view).Scroll down to the   Estimated
Earnings   section to see the amount earned during that particular date
range.How to view publisher payout information (total earnings)
Click on the  Monetization  button within the top navigation bar.Click on
 Earnings  within the dropdown that appears.All payout information should be
listed, and is available for export.Monetizing with Amplify Pre-roll - FAQs
