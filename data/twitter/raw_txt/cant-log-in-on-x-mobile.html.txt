Help with logging in to mobile.x.com
Need to reset your password?         You can send yourself a password reset
email via x.com, mobile.x.com, or SMS (if you have your mobile number connected
to your account).Learn  how to reset your password.If you're using one of our
apps and not mobile.x.com to access X, check out out the following
troubleshooting articles for our mobile apps.Help with X for iPhone or iPad Help
with X for Android        General troubleshooting         Try  clearing cache
and cookies  for your device's mobile browser.You can clear cache and cookies
from the settings menu for your mobile browser.Check to make sure that
javascript is enabled  on your device.     Other things to try         If you
are still unable to log in, please check to make sure you have the  correct
login username and password.Try logging in on a computer.If you are able to log
in on a computer but not through your mobile device's browser,  turn your phone
off  for 5 minutes to reset the connection.Share this article
