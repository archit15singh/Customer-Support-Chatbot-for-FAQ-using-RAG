How to like a Post                                Likes are represented by a
small heart and are used to show appreciation for a post.You can view the posts
you've liked from your profile page by clicking or tapping into the Likes
tab.How to like a Post                    Click or tap the  like  icon
 and it will turn red, confirming that you've liked the post.You can also like a
post from an account's profile page and a post's permalink page.To undo a like:
Click or tap the  liked  icon                               Note:  If you like
posts by accounts who have protected their posts, the update will not appear in
the  Likes  tab on your profile (unless the viewer is a follower of the post's
author).How do I see what other people have liked?        See what other people
have liked by going to their profile:   From the X for iOS or Android app:
Navigate to the profile of the account and tap  Likes.Via X.com:  Navigate to
the profile page of the account and click on  Likes  at the top of their post
timeline.Share this article                                    Post
